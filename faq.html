FAQ

Frequently asked

Can't find the answer? Reach out to us — we'll help!

What is Titanium?
Titanium is a free Lua executor for Roblox on Windows. Fast, stable, with a built-in ScriptHub and 8 themes.
How to install Titanium?
Disable your antivirus, extract the archive and run titanium.exe. Titanium launches in seconds.
Is Titanium free?
Yes! Titanium is completely free. All features are available right away with no restrictions.
What themes are available?
Titanium Dark, Neon Purple, Ocean Blue, Light, Crimson, Mint, Sunset, Monokai.
How to contact support?
Join our Telegram. We respond within minutes during working hours.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Titanium doesn't collect personal data. Your scripts and settings are stored only on your computer.
What is ScriptHub?
A built-in script library from Rscripts and Scriptblox. Browse, search, favorite, and run scripts with one click.
